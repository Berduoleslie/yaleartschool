DARKROOMS: GENERAL INFORMATION

The Yale University, School of Art Darkroom facilities are open to undergraduate or graduate student currently enrolled in the Photography Department program of study. The Undergraduate Lab consists of two multi-station B&W gang labs, finishing areas and several private multi-purpose darkrooms. There is also one 10 station digital lab for Intermediate and Advanced level classes. This lab contains Epson ink jet printers and several flat-bed scanning stations. Undergraduates enrolled in Intermediate and Advanced level classes have access to the labs during open hours.
